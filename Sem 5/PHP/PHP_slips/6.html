About Our College

Dr. D.Y. Patil Arts, Commerce & Science College, located in Pimpri, Pune, is a premier institution providing quality education in various disciplines. The college is known for its excellent academic programs, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a focus on holistic development of students.

Our college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Arts, Commerce, Science, and IT, fostering academic excellence and professional growth.
